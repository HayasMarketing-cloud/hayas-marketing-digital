## FAQs

### How long does SEO take to show results?
Typically 3-6 months for organic positioning. Combined with Google Ads, you get immediate visibility while SEO matures.

### Do you manage social media or just advertising?
Both. From content strategy and community management to advertising campaigns on Meta, LinkedIn and TikTok.

### What is AEO and GEO?
AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) are strategies for appearing in generative AI answers from ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity.

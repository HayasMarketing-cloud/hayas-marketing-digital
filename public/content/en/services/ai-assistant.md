## Service Description

### What is an AI Assistant?

An AI assistant is a custom chatbot trained on your business knowledge that can answer questions, qualify leads, and guide visitors 24/7 with human-like conversation.

### What We Include

- **Custom training**: AI trained on your products, services, and FAQs
- **Website integration**: Seamless embedding with your brand design
- **CRM connection**: Lead capture directly to your sales pipeline
- **Multilingual support**: English, Spanish, and other languages
- **Continuous learning**: Regular updates based on conversations

### Technology Stack

- **OpenAI GPT**: Advanced language understanding
- **Custom knowledge base**: Your business information
- **CRM integration**: HubSpot, Go High Level, and others
- **Analytics**: Conversation tracking and insights

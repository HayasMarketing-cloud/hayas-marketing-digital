## Service Description

### What is E-commerce Development?

E-commerce development includes the design, configuration, and optimization of a complete online store, ready to sell from day one.

### What We Include

- **UX/UI design**: Shopping experience optimized for conversion
- **Technical development**: Functional and responsive store
- **Product catalog**: Product and variant configuration
- **Payment gateways**: Stripe, PayPal, local payment methods
- **Shipping**: Carrier integration
- **E-commerce SEO**: Optimization for product searches

### Platforms We Use

- **Shopify**: Ideal for scalability and ease of use
- **WooCommerce**: Total flexibility on WordPress
- **PrestaShop**: Robust solution for large catalogs
- **Custom solutions**: Bespoke development when needed

### Our Methodology

1. **Analysis**: Products, competition, and sales objectives
2. **Design**: Conversion-oriented UX/UI
3. **Development**: Complete technical configuration
4. **Catalog**: Product upload and optimization
5. **Launch**: Testing, training, and go-live

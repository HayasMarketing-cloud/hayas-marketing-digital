## Service Description

### What is Strategic Email Marketing?

Professional email marketing goes beyond sending newsletters. It includes advanced segmentation, behavior-based automations, and continuous optimization to maximize conversions.

### What We Include

- **Email strategy**: Objectives, frequency, and email types
- **Template design**: Professional, responsive emails
- **Automations**: Welcome sequences, nurturing, abandoned cart
- **Segmentation**: Audiences based on behavior and interests
- **A/B Testing**: Continuous optimization of subjects and content
- **Reporting**: Metrics analysis and ROI

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I send emails?
Depends on sector: from 1 email/week (newsletter) to daily (promotional e-commerce).

### How do I avoid spam folders?
Good sending practices, technical authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), and value content.

### Can I import my current database?
Yes, we migrate your list with email cleaning and verification included.

### What open rates are normal?
Between 20-30% depending on sector. With good segmentation we exceed 40%.

## Service Description

### What is Content Strategy?

A content strategy defines what to create, for whom, when, and in what format. It aligns content creation with business objectives and your audience's search behavior.

### What We Include

- **Content audit**: Analysis of existing content and opportunities
- **Keyword research**: Identifying topics with search demand
- **Editorial calendar**: Monthly/quarterly publication planning
- **Content creation**: Articles, guides, infographics, videos
- **SEO optimization**: Each piece optimized for search engines

### Content Types

- **Blog posts**: Informational and thought leadership articles
- **Guides and ebooks**: Downloadable content for lead capture
- **Case studies**: Client stories with measurable results
- **Landing pages**: Conversion-optimized destination pages
- **Social content**: Adapted for each platform

### Our Methodology

1. **Audit**: Analysis of current content and competition
2. **Strategy**: Definition of pillars, formats, and calendar
3. **Creation**: High-quality content production
4. **Distribution**: Multi-channel publishing and promotion
5. **Analysis**: Results measurement and continuous optimization

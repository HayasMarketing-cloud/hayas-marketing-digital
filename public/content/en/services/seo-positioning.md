## Service Description

### What is Modern SEO?

SEO has evolved beyond Google rankings. Today's SEO includes optimizing for AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) through GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) strategies.

### What We Include

- **Technical SEO**: Site speed, structure, and crawlability
- **On-page optimization**: Content, meta tags, and schema markup
- **Link building**: Quality backlink acquisition strategy
- **GEO/AEO optimization**: Visibility in AI-generated answers
- **Content strategy**: Keyword research and content planning

### Our Three-Pillar Approach

1. **Classic SEO**: Rankings in Google and Bing
2. **AEO**: Featured snippets and direct answers
3. **GEO**: Citations in AI search results

### Our Methodology

1. **Audit**: Technical and content analysis
2. **Keyword research**: Search intent and opportunity mapping
3. **Implementation**: Technical fixes and content optimization
4. **Link building**: Authority building through quality links
5. **Monitoring**: Ranking tracking and continuous optimization

## Service Description

### What is Social Media Management?

Professional social media management includes strategy, content creation, scheduled publishing, community interaction, and results analysis.

### What We Include

- **Social strategy**: Objectives, audience, and tone of voice
- **Content calendar**: Monthly publication planning
- **Graphic design**: Creatives adapted for each platform
- **Publishing**: Post scheduling and management
- **Community management**: Response to comments and messages
- **Reporting**: Monthly results reports

### Platforms We Manage

- **LinkedIn**: B2B, thought leadership, employer branding
- **Instagram**: Visual, lifestyle, community building
- **Facebook**: Community, groups, events
- **Twitter/X**: News, conversation, support
- **TikTok**: Viral content, Gen Z engagement

### Our Methodology

1. **Audit**: Analysis of current presence and competition
2. **Strategy**: Definition of objectives, audience, and content
3. **Production**: Content and creative creation
4. **Publishing**: Daily scheduling and management
5. **Optimization**: Analysis and continuous improvement

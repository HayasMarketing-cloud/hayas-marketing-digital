## SENSE — Marketing Intelligence Platform

SENSE is Hayas Marketing's proprietary platform. It's not a methodology or a service catalog: it's a marketing intelligence platform implemented on a personalized basis for each company.

**Philosophy**: "You don't need more marketing. You need better decisions."

### How it works: Personalized implementation

SENSE is custom-configured for each company. The platform connects identity, market, demand and strategy into a continuous intelligence system. Each company works with their own marketing team, their trusted agency, or with Hayas as their execution partner.

### The 4 SENSE Intelligence Modules

#### BRAIN — Strategic Identity
Your centralized marketing memory. Value proposition, solutions, ICPs, Buyer Personas, competitive advantages and brand voice.

#### RADAR — Market Intelligence
Continuous monitoring of your industry and competitors. Prioritized signals, AI-powered reports and Market Reports.

#### SEARCH — Visibility & Intent
Keyword tracking on Google, Bing and AI engines. Brand perception audits on LLMs and Content Gap analysis.

#### STRATEGY — Strategic Direction
Market drivers, strategic directives and automatic coherence scoring between execution and identity.

### LOOP Framework
Listen → Observe → Orient → Plan. A continuous marketing intelligence cycle that feeds itself:
1. **Listen**: Monitor market signals and competitive intelligence (RADAR)
2. **Observe**: Detect patterns, opportunities and threats (SEARCH)
3. **Orient**: Define market drivers and strategic directives (STRATEGY)
4. **Plan**: Generate aligned activations with full traceability

## FAQs

### What is SENSE?
SENSE is a Marketing Operating System created by Hayas Marketing. It connects identity, market, demand, strategy and execution into a continuous intelligence flow with 4 modules (Brain, Radar, Search, Strategy) and the LOOP framework.

### What are the 6 Activations?
They are Hayas's execution units: Research, Growth, Visibility, Web & Funnel, CRM & Automation, and Content & Brand. They're not isolated services: they're connected by SENSE intelligence.

### Can I activate just one Activation?
Yes. SENSE is modular. You can start with the activation you need most and add more as you grow.

### What types of companies do you work with?
B2B and B2C companies of all sizes: from entrepreneurs to medium-sized companies with marketing teams.

### Do you implement AI in projects?
Yes. From HAYAS Copilot (proprietary AI assistant) to content automation, predictive lead scoring and generative AI tool integrations.

## Marketing SENSE - Proprietary Methodology

Marketing SENSE is Hayas Marketing's integrated methodology for designing 360° marketing plans aligned with business strategy.

**Philosophy**: "Powered by AI, governed by people"

### The 5 Phases of Marketing SENSE
1. **S - Strategy**: Diagnosis, SMART objectives, KPIs, competitive analysis
2. **E - Execution**: Service implementation, campaigns, and technical development
3. **N - Nurturing**: CRM, automation, lead scoring, email marketing
4. **S - Support**: Team training, ongoing guidance, client autonomy
5. **E - Evaluation**: Real-time dashboards, reports, continuous data-driven optimization

Marketing SENSE integrates AI across every phase as a cross-cutting tool, while maintaining human governance over strategic decisions. The result is a coherent marketing plan connecting visibility, acquisition, conversion, and retention.
